About Me

When I was 6 I had a Buzz Lightyear toy, complete with laser beams and sound effects. In my fascination, I took it apart. I destroyed that toy, but I learned a lot in the process. When I was 14, I received my first computer. Eventually, I was able to take it apart and put it back together. I'm driven by the question "How does this work?"

Gallup believes that maximimizing potential involves developing our natural talents into strengths. According to the Clifton StrengthsFinder, my #1 strength is Learning! Followed by Command, Analysis, Deliberation, and Strategy, these strengths contribute toward making informed technical decisions.

Now, with 8 years of experience working in IT, I’ve found joy in designing and implementing IT solutions that empower business, enhance workplace experience, and reduce operational cost.
