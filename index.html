Who I am

When I was 6 years old I received a Buzz Lightyear toy. You know, the one with laser beams and sound effects. I was holding some next-level science fiction in my hands and I just had to know how it worked. So I took it apart.

I'm driven by questions like "How does this work?" and "Why was it built this way?"

Gallup believes that maximizing potential involves developing our natural talents into strengths. According to the Clifton StrengthsFinder, my #1 strength is Learning, followed by Command and Analysis. I use these strengths to make informed and effective technical decisions.

The wonderful aspect of Information Technology is that it is always changing. There is always something to learn, something to improve, and something to fix.
